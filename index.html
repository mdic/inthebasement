    In the Basement

    do do do do do do... bash!

    This website was born out of the curiosity to investigate the various Basement Tapes versions I have collected over the past 25 years or so.
    I am no sound engineer nor audio expert, but I like to find out how things work and; and with the help of some Python, I set up a custom mini-application to gather technical details about each and every track in 11 different albums containing recordings from the Basement Tapes.

    Important

    This website does not contain any audio file, nor any link to audio files.
    And don't ask me where to find them.
