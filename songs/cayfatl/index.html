    Come All Ye Fair And Tender Ladies

    (title taken from BS11)

    Notes

    CB d02t10 and FTR d02t09 have an unrelated fragment at the beginning (~2 seconds).
    In addition, FTR d02t09 have an additional unrelated fragment at the end (~30 seconds guitar riffs) that on ATWR d02t04 and SBD d01t09 is included as intro to [Under Control][undc]. FTR d02t10 is [Under Control][undc], which makes the guess highly likely.
